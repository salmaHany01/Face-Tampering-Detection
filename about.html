About Us

Our mission is to provide educational tools and resources for detecting deepfakes and manipulated media. Deepfakes use artificial intelligence to create highly realistic forgeries in images, audio and video through techniques like face swaps and fake speech synthesis. As deepfake technology improves and becomes easier to use, it enables the spread of misinformation at a massive scale. We believe detecting and preventing harm from deepfakes requires public awareness of their dangers, as well as tools for fact checking and verification.

  • Comparing with known authentic media

    Comparing the image or video with known authentic media can help identify inconsistencies and differences that may indicate manipulation

  • Machine learning

    Deep learning algorithms can be trained to identify patterns in deepfake images and videos that distinguish them from real ones.

  • Anomalous facial markings

    Deepfakes sometimes exhibit unusual facial features or markings that do not occur naturally.
